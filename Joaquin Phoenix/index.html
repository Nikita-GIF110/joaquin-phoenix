Joaquin Phoenix

about

Four-time Golden Globe nominee and laureate, winner of the Volpi Cup for Best Actor at the Venice Film Festival and the Grammy Music Awards, three times Oscar and Academy Award nominees

Phoenix became world famous for the role of the Roman emperor Commodus in the film "Gladiator", also known for the films "Signs", "Hotel Rwanda", "Mysterious Forest", "Cross the Line", "She". The pinnacle of his acting skills was recognized by the overwhelming majority of critics as the role of Freddie Quell in the film The Master, for this role Phoenix almost took the Best Actor statuette at the Academy Awards.
